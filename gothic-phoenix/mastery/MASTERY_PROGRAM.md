# Gothic Phoenix 10,000-Hour Mastery Program

Start date: 2026-07-20

No prior output is retroactively credited. Passive rendering, unreviewed generation, idle compute, repeated unchanged failures, and publication without learning receive zero verified mastery credit.

## Credit rule

A practice session earns credit only when it contains:

1. a named weakness;
2. a constrained difficult exercise;
3. saved evidence;
4. formal critique;
5. a correction;
6. before/after comparison;
7. a saved lesson;
8. a delayed retest when appropriate.

Actual elapsed minutes and verified mastery minutes must be stored separately. Parallel agent time cannot be represented as elapsed human-equivalent experience.

## Stages

- Apprentice: 0–1,000
- Journeyman: 1,000–3,000
- Professional: 3,000–6,000
- Expert: 6,000–8,500
- Mastery candidate: 8,500–10,000

Hours alone never advance a stage. Each stage requires benchmark passage and sustained quality.

## Benchmark suite

Repeat at baseline, each 250 verified hours, and every stage gate:

1. Phoenix identity
2. Character continuity
3. Environment continuity
4. Material distinction
5. Emotional control
6. Composition
7. Originality
8. Video continuity
9. Commercial adaptation
10. Audience-performance prediction

## Completion standard

Completion requires 10,000 actual verified hours, all domain minimums, final benchmark average >= 90, no critical score below 85, three sustained benchmark rounds, recognizable original brand identity, commercial validation, and successful transfer to another artist system.
